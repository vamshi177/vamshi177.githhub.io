Tingo​ Tones

Add life to caller tunes

Level Up Call Experience with video ringtones

Idea Motivation

With time, people are getting more expressive on digital platforms, and the emergence of video-sharing social networking apps such as TikTok has only accelerated this trend. 

The phone call is one of the major channels that facilitates human interactions and is, therefore, one of the critical platforms where the expression of emotions matters the most.

However, the phone ringtone, which sets the stage for call conversations, hasn’t evolved much over time. A majority of phone ringtones are still audio-only which makes them less engaging, and restricts the ability to express oneself. Besides, the options available for audio ringtones are not personalized and are limited due to device space constraints. We faced these problems ourselves and are passionate to solve them.  

Features

Enable users to set personalized video ringtones

Strategy

We are building TikTok for Phone Calls. we enable our users to express themselves through phone ringtones.

Concept

We let users set mobile video ringtones. Users can also choose what video ringtone plays on their contact’s phone when they call them.

Development

We’ve launched our product on the 12th of March, 2021.
